# 🧠 DocuMind

Chat with your PDF — grounded answers with **page citations**, powered by Amazon Bedrock.

- 📄 Upload a **text-based PDF** to begin
- 🔎 Answers are retrieved from your document (RAG) and cite their sources
- 🎛️ Switch models & tune settings from the ⚙️ panel (top-right)
- ⚡ Embeddings run on Bedrock Titan; chat via the Converse API

> Tip: open the **⚙️ Settings** to pick from Kimi K2.5, DeepSeek, Mistral Large 3, GLM-5, Qwen3, gpt-oss, or Nova.
